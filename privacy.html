Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Creativity Sports Academy – Qatar collects and uses information submitted through the website registration form.

What we collect

  • Child name, age, gender, and selected sport.
  • Parent/guardian contact number (phone or WhatsApp).
  • Optional notes you submit.

Why we collect it

  • To contact you about training schedules, registrations, and academy updates.
  • To organize training groups suitable for children's ages.

How we use it

  • Information is used only for academy communication and registration management.
  • We do not sell your personal data.

Third-party services

The website uses an email delivery service to send form submissions to our email inbox. If you choose to send a copy via WhatsApp, your data will be shared with WhatsApp to deliver your message.

Children's privacy

We collect the minimum information needed to manage children's registration. Parents/guardians should submit the form.

Data retention

We keep submissions for as long as needed to manage registrations and academy communication, then delete them when no longer needed.
